What if I wanted to end my coaching engagement?

You can end your coaching engagement at any time by providing a 30-day written notice. We’ll ensure a smooth transition and wrap up any ongoing sessions or commitments within that period.

Absolutely! If you feel that your current coach isn’t the right fit, you can request a change at any time. We’ll work with you to find a coach that better suits your needs and goals.

We typically meet once a week for a 60-minute session, but this can be adjusted based on your needs and goals. Some clients prefer bi-weekly meetings, while others may need more frequent check-ins.
